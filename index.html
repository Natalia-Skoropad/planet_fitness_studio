How to stop doubting and start training regularly

Thousands of people dream about having a strong, healthy body. They start working out, face challenges, and quit after a few weeks or even days. Why does this happen? Often people push too hard when their body isn't ready. Without enough recovery, they get tired quickly and lose motivation. We believe in a smooth start and building a habit first. That's why we created easy-to-follow programs for beginners.

How to start?

  • 1

    Leave a request, call us, or visit the front desk

    We'll help you choose the club, membership, and program that match your goals and schedule.

  • 2

    Confirm your training plan and complete payment

    We activate your card, explain the rules, and show you how to use your membership to the fullest.

  • 3

    Prepare for your first workout with essentials

    Bring comfortable clothes, indoor shoes, a water bottle, and a small towel. That's all you need to start.

  • 4

    Get an introduction briefing from your trainer

    Before the first workout, the trainer will walk you through the club, show how to use the equipment, and adapt the plan to your level.

  • 5

    Enjoy your workouts and watch steady progress

    Train at your own pace, stay in touch with your coach, and celebrate every step towards a stronger, healthier you.

About Planet Fitness

“Healthy food is delicious. Working out is enjoyable. You just need to start,” people said. “Nonsense,” I thought. As a 19-year-old guy, overweight and exhausted, living on junk food to handle stress, those healthy-life gurus sounded like aliens from another planet. On their planet, running felt easy. On mine, pizza felt normal and jogging was torture.

Twelve years later, after losing almost 100 pounds and completely changing my lifestyle, I still believe we were on different planets. The difference is that I managed to escape. Today I am a coach with 7+ years of experience and the founder of a chain of clubs where fitness bars, psychologists, and dietitians work together. Planet Fitness is your spacecraft: the gym is the cabin, the bar helps your body adapt, and our friendly crew keeps you motivated along the way.
